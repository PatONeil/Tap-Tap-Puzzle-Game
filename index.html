Instructions

Objective of Game

The objective of the game is to eliminate all of the cubes. The cubes can only be move in the direction of the arrows on the cube.

If a cube can be moved off the display area without being blocked by another cube, tap it to remove it.

Pan, Zoom and Rotate Cube

The cubes can be rotated by moving a single finger touch moving around the display area

The cubes can be moved around the display area with a two finger touch.

The cubes can be zoomed with a pinch move with two fingers in the display area.
